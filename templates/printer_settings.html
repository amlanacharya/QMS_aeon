Bluetooth Printer Settings

Enter the MAC address of your default Bluetooth printer. This will be used for direct printing via QR code.
How to Find Your Printer's MAC Address
  1. Go to your device's Bluetooth settings
  2. Find the printer in the list of paired devices
  3. On most devices, tap on the settings icon next to the printer name
  4. The MAC address will be displayed (format: XX:XX:XX:XX:XX:XX)
