Setup Instructions
Before Testing:
  1. Install the Bluetooth Print app from Google Play Store
  2. Connect your thermal printer to your Android device via Bluetooth or USB
  3. Open the app and enable "Browser Print" function in the settings
  4. Click on the test button above
